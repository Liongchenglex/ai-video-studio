Design intent:

  • No separate "steps." Script, voice, and storyboard are one screen. The old stepper shrinks to a short first-run setup (style + brief), then you live here.
  • "Generate all" (top-right) is the directing move — batch every image/clip, watch a queue, review thumbnails. Per-shot buttons stay in the inspector for fixes.
  • Bible on the left is always visible so consistency isn't a thing you forget to do.
